Movies and Series Reviews according to Abi

...what I watched in 2024

Series

House of Dragon

ongoing

Intense, entertaining. Prequel to the Games of Thrones Series. I would give it a 7/10

Our Blues

It's a korean drama and that's what it is: A Drama. It has good character development for the kdrama standards and you can see some of the stereotypes and prejudices the society still has. I would give it a 6/10 which is high for its category.

The Last of Us

ongoing

I didn't play the videogame but I watched gameplays on youtube several years ago. I was a fan and still am. I was a bit disappointed with the end but that's a very subjective opinion. 8/10

Movies

Everything, Everywhere, all at once

This movie is kind of A Lot. It's entertaining and crazy but it can get to a point where you just say wtf. Some jokes are not necessary and it feels super long. But it has some nice quotes and life lessons I guess. 5/10

Don't move

Nerve racking. I don't enjoy these kinds of movies for a good reason. It's predictable in the general, but it has some peculiarities that you don't expect. I remember everything so, at least it's memorable. 7/10

Wicked

part 1

Despite all the Ariana Grande drama, the movie was quite enjoyable, although I did watch it in a 3 day lapse. I'm expecting the sequel and I started reading the Wizard of Oz because of it. Nothing extra but entertaining. 7/10
