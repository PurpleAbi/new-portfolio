I'm a year old mom who is learning.

To code. About technology. About data. To build.

At 16 I had 2 choices:
Either I entered an elite high school with 50% scholarship.
OR
I completely devoted myself to arts and prepare to be a professional dancer.
I chose the later.

After completing my college studies in a well known contemporary dance school in Mexico I felt like something was missing, like my essence didn't completely match the industry. I felt lost. It took me 8 years to find myself again. I don't regret choosing dance, because that path, although long, directed me to where I am today and to ...

coding.


So here I am.
